Our Certification & Philosophy

A doula is a trained professional who provides continuous physical, emotional, and informational support to a birthing person before, during, and after childbirth. We are not medical professionals, but we are trained to work side-by-side with clinical teams in a variety of care settings to support your informed choices and comfort.

My Commitment to You

I am a certified doula with Partum Health and expect to be covered by the State of Illinois Medicaid in 2026. I have completed rigorous training, attended multiple births, and finished comprehensive coursework in prenatal, labor, and postpartum support.

My practice is grounded in evidence-based information, compassionate care, and respectful communication. Most private insurance does not cover doula services because doulas are not considered medically necessary. I can accept benefits from Carrot Fertility. Most clients pay for services on a sliding scale.

My training includes birth preference planning, labor comfort measures, breathing techniques, partner support, newborn care basics, and community resource connections. I am dedicated to continuing education to ensure I provide the highest level of care to my clients.
